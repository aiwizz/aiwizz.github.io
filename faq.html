Installing Recap Dat is easy! Simply visit our download page and click on the button for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari). You'll be redirected to your browser's extension store where you can add it with one click. The installation takes less than 10 seconds.
Once installed, navigate to any article or web page you want to summarize. Click the Recap Dat icon in your browser toolbar, and our AI will instantly generate a concise summary. You can choose between bullet points, paragraph format, or key insights depending on your preference.
Recap Dat is available on all major browsers: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. We ensure consistent performance and features across all supported browsers.
